High-Quality Laminated Veneer Lumber (LVL)

Laminated veneer lumber (LVL) is made from layers of selected timber species which are heat laminated together. Laminated veneer lumber is stronger, more durable, and can bear more weight than ordinary timber lumber. Treated Pine

We are able to order all sizes of Treated Pine and have stock available for purchase

Treated pine is a timber that has been treated with chemical solutions containing both fungicide and insecticides, which aims to provide long-term resistance to decay and insects. The treatment is an effective protection which makes the treated pine durable even in applications where it is exposed to the elements.

Rougher Headed is usually for construction and landscaping.

It can be supplied in H2, H3, H4, H5, H6

H2 treated pine is suitable to the interior, above-ground use.

H3 treated pine is suitable for exterior and interior above-ground use.

H4 treated pine is suitable for exterior, underground use.

Hazard Level Exposure Specific Conditions Hazard Typical Uses
H1 Inside, above ground Completely protected fro the weather and well ventilated and protected from termites Lyctid Borer Framing, flooring, furniture, interior joinery
