Services

Milling

Rallis Timber can recreate an existing profile in timber – milled to order. We have a huge range of existing profiles in MDF, Timber and Primed Timber, but can also recreate a profile if you bring us a sample.


Moulding

We stock a wide variety of timber moulding profiles, including architraves, skirting boards, floorboards, beading, picture rails, fancy moulds, door jambs, dowels, and window profiles. Our door jambs are also available in Pine, Western Red Cedar, or Pacific Maple, whilst our architraves and floorboards are also available in MDF.

Custom work

We specialise in matching and replicating all types of moulding profiles. This is perfect for restoring older houses, replacing a damaged moulding, or matching moulding you like from a different house. Contact us today or visit us in store with a sample of the moulding you would like to match, and our friendly staff will be able to assist you.
